# CO2-solubility-dataset
This dataset comprises 624 samples, covering CO2 solubility in 11 different physical solvents across a temperature range of 288.15 to 318.15 K and a pressure range of 0.058 to 5.92 MPa.

References：

[1] X. Gui, Z. Tang, W. Fei, Solubility of co2 in alcohols, glycols, ethers, and ketones at high pressures from (288.15
to 318.15) k, Journal of Chemical & Engineering Data 56 (5) (2011) 2420–2429.

[2] V. Mehtab, S. Alam, S. Povari, L. Nakka, Y. Soujanya, S. Chenna, Reduced order machine learning models
for accurate prediction of co2 capture in physical solvents, Environmental science & technology 57 (46) (2023)
18091–18103.
